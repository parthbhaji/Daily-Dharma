Frequently Asked Questions

How does the daily verse work?

Daily Dharma features a new shloka every day of the year — 365 verses across five Hindu scriptures. The verse rotates automatically each day and is the same for all users.

How do I reset my streak?

Your streak increments each day you open the app. If you miss a day, it resets to 1. Simply open the app every day to maintain it.

How do I change my display name?

Tap the profile icon in the top right corner of the Today screen. Then tap "Edit Name" to update your display name shown in Satsang.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at support@dailydharma.app with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will process your request within 7 days.

The app isn't loading. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. If you still experience problems, please contact us at support@dailydharma.app.
