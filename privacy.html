Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

Daily Dharma ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Display name — the name shown in the Satsang community section
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one (optional)
  • Saved verses and reflections — content you choose to save or post in the app
  • Usage data — streak count and last active date

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Daily Dharma service
  • Display your name and photo in the Satsang community
  • Save your verse collection and streak progress across devices
  • Send daily push notifications with the verse of the day (if you opt in)
  • Respond to support requests

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud platform compliant with industry security standards. Your data is stored in the United States.

Community Content

Reflections you post in the Satsang section are visible to other users of the app. Your display name will appear alongside your posts. Please do not include personal information in your public reflections.

Push Notifications

If you grant permission, we will send you a daily push notification containing the verse of the day. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.

Third-Party Services

Daily Dharma uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage
  • Expo — push notification delivery

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at support@dailydharma.app with the subject "Delete My Account". We will process your request within 7 days.

Children's Privacy

Daily Dharma is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
